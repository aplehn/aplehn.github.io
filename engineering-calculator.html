Engineering Calculator

Formulas and approaches for solving engineering problems such as preliminary stress estimates, stress concentrations, and fatique analyses can have a plethora of associated equations which operate under separate assumptions at separate points in the problem solving process. Having a programmed framework for quickly and reliably performing hand calcluatons can be a major time saver during real world projects. This calculator uses a series of functions separated into files by subject. A homework problem or preliminary calculation can be performed by passing relevant inputs into these functions within a problem file step by step. This approach has saved me hours on homework assignments and project decison making.
